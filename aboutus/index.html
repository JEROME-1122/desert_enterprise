ABOUT US

Desert Enterprises is one of Oman’s leading diversified resource companies, dedicated to responsible mining and mineral development. We produce essential minerals that enhance the quality of life globally, including steel-grade limestone, cement-grade limestone, high-purity gypsum, agricultural gypsum, laterite, kaolin clay, and attapulgite clay.

Based in Salalah, Oman, we operate or hold stakes in five quarries across the country. In addition to our existing operations, we are actively exploring new industrial mineral opportunities in Oman and Africa.

With a rich legacy in mining, Desert Enterprises has earned a reputation as a trusted mineral producer. As one of the oldest mineral producers in Oman, we are proud to be a growing family-owned business, continuing to build on our tradition of excellence.

CORPORATE GOVERNANCE

Employees and Directors owe the Company a duty to advance the Company's business interests when the opportunity to do so arises. As a result, Employees and Directors are prohibited from taking personal advantage of certain business opportunities in which the Company may be interested. This so-called "corporate opportunity doctrine" is complicated and it is not possible to clearly define all of the business opportunities which belong or could be of interest to the Company and what business opportunities may be taken advantage of personally by Employees or Directors.
